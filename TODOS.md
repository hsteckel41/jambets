## Tease Rulebook / Convention Guide

**What:** Surface a convention guide in the market creation flow that nudges creators to specify edge case rules in their question text. E.g., "Does a tease count? Specify in your question!" tooltip.

**Why:** Jam band markets will generate disputes about whether a tease counts as a song being played, whether a song that segues into another counts, etc. The app can't adjudicate these — but it can help creators avoid the dispute entirely by front-loading the ruling in the question.

**Pros:** Reduces dispute rate. Educates new users on common edge cases. Could include a floating "tease rulebook" reference users can cite.

**Cons:** Adds UX complexity to the create market flow. Could be annoying if over-prominent.

**Context:** This came up during office hours — the user immediately named "teases" and "song segues" as edge cases they've personally argued about in group chats. Dispute rate target is under 20%.

**Depends on:** Market creation flow being built first. No blockers.
